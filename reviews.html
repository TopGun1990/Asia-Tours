    • Thailand tour

    Interesting tour across Thailand

    Freedom Beach in Phuket is a little part of paradise a longtail boat ride from Patong Beach. You can get there by car but the walkway down to the beach is quite steep and tricky if you have kids or loads to carry. The beach is absolutely beautiful. Crystal clear water, white sands, palm trees with food & drink stalls. We spent a few hours lazing and swimming here. Not too busy especially in June. We caught a Longtail home to Patong and was a great day out. Definitely recommended.

  • Kate Peters
    • Monkey Island tour

    Enjoyed Monkey Island tour so much!

    I have to admit that after reading so many poor reviews of Monkey Island, we booked accommodation elsewhere, but upon arriving at Koh Rong and seeing the bungalows right along the water, quickly decided to stay at Monkey Island and loved it. Staff were so helpful, food was amazing and our bungalow, though basic was great. We paid a little more to stay in a bungalow with private bathroom which was definitely worth it for our family. We stayed at a different bungalow the following night which was cheaper but discovered that paying the little extra at monkey island got us additional comforts that other places lacked...clean bedding, untorn mosquito nets, towels, mirrors and being so close to the jetty (which makes a difference when carrying enormous packs). There was an occassional smell from bathrooms, but it quickly disappeared.

    November 2019
